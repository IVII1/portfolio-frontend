About me

I am a full-stack web developer based in Podgorica, Montenegro. I've been consistently programming for 2 years in my spare time. I started out with front-end development but ended up really liking interacting with APIs in the process. I was very interested in how they work so I started learning back-end development. Other than coding, I enjoy gaming, sports and all things related to tech, especially PCs and HiFi.
